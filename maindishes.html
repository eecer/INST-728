Shirataki Chicken Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 1 package shirataki noodles
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions:

  • Preheat your skillet with olive oil
  • Stir fry small cubed chicken breast
  • Add drained shirataki noodles and stir fry for 2 minutes
  • Mix in the heavy whipping cream and spices
