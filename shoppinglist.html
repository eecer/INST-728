Shopping List

A Typical Shopping List!

Planning is everything when it comes to your diet.One of the best plans you can make is a Keto shopping list. Pretty soon you will memorize this list, knowing which foods make you feel the best and fill you up the longest on your Keto diet.

When I go shopping, I usually buy these for sure:

  • Butter
  • Coconut oil
  • MCT oil for bulletproof coffee
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Eggs
  • Nuts
  • Cheese
  • Avocados
  • Chicken/fish/beef
  • Cauliflower
  • Berries
